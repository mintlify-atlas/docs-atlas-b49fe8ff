## Common questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about Letter Generator pricing and billing.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's included in the free tier?">
    The free tier includes generous daily letter generations with access to all core features:
    
    - Daily generation limit (resets every 24 hours)
    - All 25+ letter types
    - All 17 tone options
    - Multilingual support
    - Full editing and download capabilities
    - No credit card required
    
    The free tier is perfect for occasional letter writing and trying out the service.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How many letters can I generate per day on the free tier?">
    The free tier includes a generous daily limit that allows you to generate multiple letters each day. This limit resets every 24 hours, giving you fresh generations daily.
    
    If you need to generate more letters than the daily limit allows, consider upgrading to a premium plan for unlimited access.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when I reach my daily limit?">
    When you reach your daily generation limit on the free tier, you have two options:
    
    1. **Wait for the reset** - Your limit automatically resets after 24 hours
    2. **Upgrade to premium** - Get unlimited generations immediately
    
    You'll receive a notification when you're approaching your daily limit.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does premium billing work?">
    Premium plans provide unlimited letter generations with flexible billing:
    
    - Simple, transparent pricing
    - Automatic renewal (can be cancelled anytime)
    - Access to all current and future features
    - Priority customer support
    
    You can view and manage your subscription at any time through your account settings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade from free to premium at any time?">
    Yes! You can upgrade to a premium plan whenever you need unlimited access:
    
    - Upgrade instantly from your account
    - No migration needed - keep all your settings
    - Start generating unlimited letters immediately
    - Cancel anytime if your needs change
    
    Your premium access begins as soon as you complete the upgrade.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I downgrade from premium to free?">
    Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time. After cancellation:
    
    - You'll retain premium access until the end of your billing period
    - Your account automatically converts to the free tier
    - You'll have access to the generous daily generations
    - All your previous letters remain accessible
    
    There are no penalties or fees for downgrading.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    Refund policies depend on your specific situation:
    
    - Contact our support team if you're unsatisfied with premium
    - We'll work with you to find a solution
    - Many issues can be resolved without refunding
    
    Please reach out to support with any billing concerns.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a discount for annual billing?">
    Pricing and billing options may vary. For information about:
    
    - Annual billing discounts
    - Team or enterprise plans
    - Custom pricing for high-volume needs
    
    Please visit [lettergenerator.co](https://lettergenerator.co/) or contact our support team.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card for the free tier?">
    No! The free tier requires no payment information whatsoever:
    
    - Start generating letters immediately
    - No credit card required
    - No trial period to worry about
    - No automatic charges
    
    You only provide payment information if you choose to upgrade to premium.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are there different premium plan tiers?">
    Letter Generator offers premium plans designed for unlimited access. For details about:
    
    - Specific plan tiers and pricing
    - Features included in each tier
    - Business or enterprise options
    
    Visit [lettergenerator.co](https://lettergenerator.co/) to view current pricing and plan options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

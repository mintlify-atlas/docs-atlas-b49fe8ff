Get up and running with Letter Generator in just a few simple steps. This guide will walk you through creating your first letter from start to finish.

## Create your first letter

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Letter Generator">
    Visit [lettergenerator.co](https://lettergenerator.co/) in your web browser. No sign-up is required to start generating letters with the free tier.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your letter type">
    Choose from over 25 different letter types to match your specific need:
    - **Professional**: Cover letters, resignation letters, job applications
    - **Personal**: Thank you notes, apology letters, love letters
    - **Formal**: Complaint letters, request letters, business correspondence
    - **Academic**: Recommendation letters, admission letters, scholarship applications
    
    Click on the letter type that best fits your purpose.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your tone">
    Select the tone that matches your intended message from 17 available options:
    - Professional, Formal, Friendly, Persuasive
    - Empathetic, Assertive, Diplomatic, Enthusiastic
    - Romantic, Casual, Respectful, and more
    
    The AI will adapt the language and style to match your chosen tone.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter letter details">
    Fill in the required information:
    - **Recipient name**: Who the letter is addressed to
    - **Purpose**: The main reason for writing the letter
    - **Additional details**: Any specific points, achievements, or context you want to include
    
    <Tip>
      Be specific with your details. The more context you provide, the better and more personalized your generated letter will be.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate your letter">
    Click the **Generate** button and watch as AI creates your letter in seconds. The AI analyzes your inputs and crafts a well-structured, contextually appropriate letter.
    
    <Note>
      Free tier users can generate several letters per day. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited generations.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and use your letter">
    Once generated, you can:
    - **Copy** the letter to your clipboard
    - **Edit** the content directly in the interface
    - **Download** as a document file
    - **Generate again** with modified inputs if needed
    
    Review the letter and make any personal touches before sending.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cultural considerations

The AI adapts letters to cultural norms:

### Formality levels

Different cultures have different expectations for formality:

- **High formality cultures** - The AI uses more formal structures and respectful language
- **Moderate formality** - Balances professional distance with approachability
- **Lower formality** - Allows for more casual, direct communication where culturally appropriate

<Note>
The AI considers cultural context when generating letters, but you can adjust the tone to match your specific needs.
</Note>

### Greeting and closing conventions

Letters adapt to language-specific conventions:

- **Greetings** - Uses appropriate salutations for each language and culture
- **Closings** - Applies conventional sign-offs that match the letter's formality
- **Titles and honorifics** - Includes proper titles where culturally expected
- **Address formats** - Structures recipient information according to local norms

### Content organization

Some cultures prefer different organizational approaches:

- **Direct vs. indirect** - Adjusts how directly the main point is stated
- **Context level** - Provides appropriate background and context
- **Emotional expression** - Calibrates emotional content to cultural norms
- **Persuasion style** - Adapts argumentative approaches to cultural preferences

<Tip>
If you're familiar with the target culture's communication preferences, you can fine-tune the AI output by selecting specific tones or providing cultural context in your details.
</Tip>

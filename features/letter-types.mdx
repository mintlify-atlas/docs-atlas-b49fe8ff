Letter Generator supports over 25 different letter types, covering everything from formal business correspondence to personal messages. Each letter type is optimized with AI to match the conventions and expectations of that specific format.

## How to select a letter type

When you start creating a new letter, you'll choose your letter type first:

1. Navigate to the letter generation interface
2. Browse or search through the available letter types
3. Select the type that matches your communication goal
4. Provide specific details relevant to that letter type
5. Generate your letter with AI optimization for that format

<Info>
The AI automatically adjusts structure, formality, and content based on your selected letter type to ensure your message follows best practices.
</Info>

## What happens after selection

Once you select a letter type:

1. **Customized prompts** - You'll receive specific fields relevant to that letter type
2. **AI optimization** - The AI applies format and style conventions for that category
3. **Structure guidance** - Your letter follows the expected structure for that type
4. **Content suggestions** - The AI includes elements commonly expected in that format

<Note>
All letter types work seamlessly with tone selection and multilingual support for maximum flexibility.
</Note>
